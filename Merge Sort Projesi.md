
# Merge Sort Projesi

					      |16| |21| |11| <>  |8| |12| |22|  
						*			*
					 |16|<>|21| |11|             |8|<> |12| |22|
					        **			  **
					|16|    |21|<> |11|           |8|    |12|<>|22|
						***			   ***
					|16|    |21|   <>  |11|	     |8|    |12|  <>  |22|
					        ****			    ****
					|16| <>	|11||21|	      |8| <> |12||22|
					        *****			   *****
					|11||16||21|		         |8||12||22|
								*****
							|8||11||12||16||21||22|
her satırda n işlem 

2^x = n 
x = logn 
Big-o = O(nlogn) Lineer
